-----------------------------------------------
A top down shooter game based on a rogue detective fighting corruption in his hometown.
-------------------CONTROLS--------------------
W - Move Up
S - Move Down
A - Move Left 
D - Move Right
Shift - Sprint
R - Reload 
Right mouse button - Shoot
Move mouse to aim
~ - Debug Console *commands not yet fully implemented*
------------*Yet To be implemented*------------
Mouse Wheel or Q/E - Change weapons
F - Use object, Open door
1, 2, 3,...9 - Skills
Space - Skip text/ Skip cutscene
